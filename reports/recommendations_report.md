# Recommendations Report

**Annual Context**: The organization incurs **$3.5M in annual attrition cost**. A 20% reduction through targeted interventions could save approximately **$694K**. The Workforce Health Score of 56/100 signals systemic risk requiring strategic focus.

## Priority 1: Sales Department Overhaul
- **Issue**: 39.3% attrition rate, highest in company
- **Root Causes**: High overtime (28.6%), average satisfaction (3.29), career growth concerns
- **Actions**:
  - Revise compensation structure with performance-based bonuses
  - Limit mandatory overtime to maximum 10 hours/week
  - Implement monthly recognition program
  - Create clear 12-month career progression path
  - Assign mentors to all new sales hires
- **Expected Impact**: -30% attrition in Sales within 2 quarters
- **Cost/Effort**: Medium

## Priority 2: Career Path Framework Implementation
- **Issue**: 20.5% of attrition is due to career growth concerns
- **Root Causes**: Opaque promotion criteria, inconsistent review cycles
- **Actions**:
  - Establish transparent promotion criteria for every role
  - Implement quarterly career development reviews
  - Create mentorship program pairing juniors with senior leaders
  - Allocate annual training budget per employee ($2,000 minimum)
  - Fast-track promotion process for high performers
- **Expected Impact**: -40% promotion-related attrition
- **Cost/Effort**: Low-Medium

## Priority 3: Work-Life Balance Initiatives
- **Issue**: 28.2% of attrition is due to WLB concerns (top reason)
- **Root Causes**: High overtime, lack of flexible options, no wellness programs
- **Actions**:
  - Introduce flexible working hours for all departments
  - Enable remote work options (2-3 days/week)
  - Implement mandatory maximum overtime policy
  - Launch Employee Assistance Program (mental health support)
  - Conduct quarterly satisfaction pulse surveys
- **Expected Impact**: Increase avg satisfaction from 3.4 to 4.0 in 3 quarters
- **Cost/Effort**: Low

## Priority 4: High-Performer Retention
- **Issue**: High performers without recent promotions are flight risks
- **Root Causes**: No recognition, stagnant career growth despite strong performance
- **Actions**:
  - Identify and flag all high performers (rating >= 4) without promotion in 2+ years
  - Create accelerated growth tracks for top talent
  - Provide equity/stock options for key contributors
  - Assign leadership visibility projects
  - Implement stay interviews (quarterly)
- **Expected Impact**: Retain 95% of high-performing employees
- **Cost/Effort**: Medium-High

## Priority 5: Operations Department Intervention
- **Issue**: 33.3% attrition, highest overtime rate (50%), lowest engagement (2.78)
- **Actions**:
  - Reduce overtime through better shift planning
  - Increase engagement through team activities and recognition
  - Improve promotion rate (currently 5.6%)
  - Review manager effectiveness in the department
- **Expected Impact**: Reduce Operations attrition by 25%
- **Cost/Effort**: Medium
